Tutorial: How to Use the Tool



  1. Step 1: Open Instagram through website
  2. Step 2: Go to Settings
  3. Step 3: Go to Privacy and Security
  4. Step 4: Click on "Request download" in Data download
  5. Step 5: Select "JSON" as the Information format and Request Download
  6. Step 6: Download the file from the email sent to you by Instagram
  7. Step 7: Extract all folders from the zip file
  8. Step 8: In the Instagram Unfollower Finder tool. Choose the followers and following file from the extracted folder named "followers_and_following"
  9. Step 9: Click the "Compare" button
  10. Step 10: Review the output

The tutorial is the same for mobile phones and PCs

Note: Instagram should be opened on website as you cannot request your data on the mobile app
