Tutorial: How to Use the Tool


Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Open Instagram on Phone or Website
  • Go to Settings
  • Go to Accounts Centre
  • Go to Your information and permissions
  • Click on "Download your information"
  • Click on "Some of your information" in Download information
  • Select "Followers and Following" in the Connections Menu
  • Select "Download to device, Date range: All time and JSON format" then Create files
  • Wait a couple of minutes for it to be processed
  • Download the file Instagram sends to you through your email that says the requested file is ready for download
  • Extract all files from the zip folder
  • In the Instagram Unfollower Finder tool, choose the followers and following file from the Extracted folder named "connections"
  • Click the "Compare" button
  • Review the output

The tutorial is the same for mobile phones and PCs
